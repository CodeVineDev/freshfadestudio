Our Process

A refined 6-step process that removes uncertainty

01 • Discovery

Private consultation, scope & site review. We define outcomes, budget range & timeline.

02 • Concept

Mood boards, layouts & 3D visuals so you know exactly what to expect before build commences.

03 • Execution

Procurement, contractor coordination & on-site management with weekly updates.

04 • Finishing

Lighting, art placement & styling to reveal the final composition.

05 • Handover

Final walkthrough, documentation & care guide for your home.

06 • Aftercare

Optional maintenance & refresh services to keep the space immaculate through seasons.
